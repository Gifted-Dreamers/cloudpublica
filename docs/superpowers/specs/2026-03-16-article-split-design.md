## Design Decisions

1. **The Endgame is the flagship** — it's the deeper analysis with the ontology framing. The Lookup Table is more accessible but The Endgame is the full picture.
2. **Shared sections** — Legal Record, AI Chat History, Methodology, Appendix appear in both but with scope differences. The Lookup Table has the core cases; The Endgame adds education litigation.
3. **Cross-references are inline** — woven into body text like the Psychology article does, not just footer links. Each article references the other in its opening paragraph.
4. **Domain detail split** — The Lookup Table has full detail on data infrastructure, surveillance table, legal architecture, election control, oversight, financial architecture. The Endgame has full detail on Miller, ontology architecture, cognitive warfare, education, healthcare, BITE mapping.
5. **Diagrams are regenerated** — using the frontend-design skill for HTML/CSS/SVG diagrams that match the site's light theme, rather than the old PNG mermaid renders.
6. **Friend's byline** — placeholder in The Lookup Table, Kristine adds later.
