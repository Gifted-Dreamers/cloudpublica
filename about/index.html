About Cloud Publica

We publish investigations and research focused on the structural analysis of power, surveillance, and public infrastructure.

What We Do

We investigate the systems that shape public life but operate beyond public scrutiny: surveillance technologies, AI companionship harms, authoritarian manipulation tactics, financial networks, and the tools that can make these structures visible.

Our work encompasses three main pillars:

Investigations

Deep structural analyses sourced from court records, FOIA responses, corporate filings, and government databases.

Research

Literature reviews and original analysis at the intersection of technology, psychology, and power.

Tools

Open-source transparency tools designed for journalists, researchers, and the public to track institutional power.

Who We Are

Cloud Publica is an independent project of Gifted Dreamers, Inc., a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Austin, Texas (EIN 39-3863796).

Gifted Dreamers builds community-powered tools for resilience, transparency, and civic participation. Our sister project, justNICE.us, provides Know Your Rights resources and immigrant community support infrastructure.

Our Principles

No ads. No tracking.

We never run ads, use analytics trackers, or set cookies. Your reading behavior is entirely private.

No paywalls.

All investigations are free and will remain free. We believe public interest research should be accessible to the public.

Show your sources.

Every claim we make links directly to its primary source material. Court filings, government databases, and public records—verify everything for yourself.

Structural, not sensational.

We analyze underlying systems and persistent patterns, not passing controversies or personalities. The structure itself is the story.
