About Cloud Publica

Cloud Publica is an investigations and research platform focused on structural analysis of power, surveillance, and public infrastructure. We publish long-form investigations, research, and tools — all sourced from public records, court filings, and government documents.

What We Do

We investigate the systems that shape public life but operate beyond public scrutiny: surveillance technologies, AI companionship harms, authoritarian manipulation tactics, financial networks, and the tools that can make these structures visible.

Our work includes:

  • Investigations — Deep structural analyses sourced from court records, FOIA responses, corporate filings, and government databases.
  • Research — Literature reviews and original analysis at the intersection of technology, psychology, and power.
  • Tools — Open-source transparency tools for journalists, researchers, and the public.

Who We Are

Cloud Publica is a project of Gifted Dreamers, Inc., a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization based in Austin, Texas (EIN 39-3863796).

Gifted Dreamers builds community-powered tools for resilience, transparency, and civic participation. Our sister project, justNICE.us, provides Know Your Rights resources and immigrant community support.

Our Principles

No ads. No tracking.

We don't run ads, use analytics trackers, or set cookies. Your reading is private.

No paywalls.

All investigations are free and will remain free. Public interest research should be public.

Show your sources.

Every claim links to its source. Court filings, public records, government databases — verify for yourself.

Structural, not sensational.

We analyze systems and patterns, not personalities. The structure is the story.
